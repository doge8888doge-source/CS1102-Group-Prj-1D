What is Synthetic Identity Fraud?

  • A fabricated identity using mixed real + fake data

  • Fraudsters build credit history over time

  • Hard to detect due to no single red flag

Industry Impact

  • Responsible for billions in annual losses

  • Traditional systems miss relational fraud

  • Key challenges:

    • Hidden connections between identities

    • Evolving fraud tactics

    • Balancing detection and false positives

    • Compliance with fair lending laws
